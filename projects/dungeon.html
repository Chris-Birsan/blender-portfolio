Dungeon Environment

3D Environment | Blender | 2024

About This Project

An atmospheric dungeon environment created in Blender, featuring detailed texturing, dramatic lighting, and a moody ambiance. This project focused on environmental storytelling and creating a believable game-ready scene.

Techniques Used

  • Low-poly modeling with efficient topology
  • PBR texturing with color, roughness, and normal maps
  • Atmospheric lighting with torches and ambient occlusion
  • Particle effects for dust and atmosphere

Tools & Software

  • Blender 4.5 - Modeling and rendering
  • Cycles - Volumetric rendering
